About Me

Growing up my mother would allways take photos whenever and wherever. Back then I hated how she would document everything, but as I've grown older It has apeared to have rubbed of on me. Nowadays I find myself taking photos whenever I can and doing so has helped me immensely improve.

I still have a long way to go, but I hope that you will join me on this journey!
